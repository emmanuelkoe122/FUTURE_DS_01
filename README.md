# FUTURE_DS_01_Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a real-world retail dataset to extract meaningful business insights. The goal is to simulate the role of a data analyst by cleaning raw transactional data, performing in-depth analysis, and presenting actionable recommendations that support business decision-making. This process includes several stages: data processing, data cleaning, data analysis, and data visualization.

**Dataset used:**
- Superstore Sales Dataset

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vivek468/superstore-dataset-final

---

## Objectives
The main objective of this project is to answer key business questions such as:

1. Which products and categories generate the most revenue?
2. How do sales and profit change over time?
3. Which categories or regions are most profitable?
4. Where should the business focus to maximize growth?

---

## Tools & Technologies
This project was completed using:

1. **Microsoft Excel** → Data cleaning, preprocessing, pivot tables and dashboard creation
2. **Excel Charts** → Data visualization and dashboard design
3. **CSV / XLSX** → Data storage and export

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation
Before analysis, the dataset was cleaned and prepared using Excel:

1. Removed missing values and duplicates
2. Converted data types (dates, numeric fields)
3. Standardized text fields (regions, categories, segments)
4. Created new features — Margin feature (Profit / Sales)
5. Aggregated data using Pivot Tables by Year, Category, Region, Segment and Sub-Category

---

## Analysis Performed

1. **Revenue & Profit Trends Over Time**
   - Analyzed yearly revenue and profit evolution from 2014 to 2017 using Pivot Tables

2. **Top-Selling Products & Sub-Categories**
   - Identified best and worst performing sub-categories by revenue and margin

3. **High-Value Regions**
   - Compared the four US regions by revenue, profit and margin

4. **Customer Segment Analysis**
   - Evaluated Consumer, Corporate and Home Office segment profitability

## Key Insights
- Total revenue reached **$2.29M** over 4 years with a net profit of **$285,988** (12% margin)
- Revenue grew by **+51%** from 2014 to 2017; profit grew even faster at **+89%**
- **Technology** is the most profitable category (17.4% margin)
- **Furniture** is the weakest category — Tables and Bookcases are loss-making sub-categories
- The **West region** leads in both revenue and profitability (14.9% margin)
- The **Central region** significantly underperforms with only 7.9% margin
- The **Home Office** segment has the highest margin (14%) but remains underexploited

---

## Business Recommendations
- Stop excessive discounting on Tables and Bookcases — both are sold at a loss
- Audit the Central region's discount practices and product mix
- Scale up sales of Copiers and Accessories — top margins, underexploited volume
- Develop the Home Office segment with targeted product bundles
- Protect and reinforce the West and East regions — they generate 70% of total profit
- Implement monthly margin tracking by sub-category to detect issues early
